FAQ

Transfers are rides that you and your crew take from the airport to your accommodation. Normally, you meet the driver outside of the arrival hall of the airport. He will pick-up and load your luggage in the vehicle. Then, he will take you to your hotel/ destination.

Our airport transportation services are exclusively private for your family or crew. We do not offer shared transfers at the moment because of Covid-19 regulations.

A transfer for 2 people in Mauritius normally costs around â‚¬30 to â‚¬50. These rates also depend on the destination and number of people to carry.

The quantity of luggage you can bring is determined by the number of people traveling and the vehicle you are in. By default, each passenger is allowed one suitcase and one bag. We recognize that you may have extra luggage so in that situation, please clarify your needs when making your reservation, and an appropriate ride dedicated to your needs will be assigned to you. If you require baby seats, please let us know beforehand.

There is no payment in advance for any of our transfers. Cancellations and changes can be made by email at info@taxiairportmauritius.com or contacting us at +230 5473 3484. For more information, contact us directly.!

Proceed to the exit (of the arrival lounge) after completing customs. Your driver will be waiting for you here for the pick-up. He'll be carrying a board or paper with your name printed on it. Simply provide him with your electronic receipt or booking reference, and he will direct you to your vehicle.

We do not take payment and do not charge for cancellations. We do request a confirmation 48 hours before your arrival, just notify us then.

We track airplane arrival times on a daily basis and arrange accodingly. Please provide us with your flight details for us to track your arrival schedule after requesting a quote.

After you get to your location, you will pay your driver in cash. You can pay in dollars, euros, pounds and mauritian rupees. If you schedule numerous excursions with us, we advise you to just pay the percentage of the price that applies to the tour you take.

We offer multiple sightseeing tours and activities around the island. The sightseeing tours include North Tour, South West Tour, South East Tour and Port Louis Tour. The activities include Catamaran And Swim With Dolphins. Our chauffeurs will show you a booklet of the best spots during your airport transfer.
